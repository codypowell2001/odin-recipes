Naan Bread and Hummus

Description:

This recipe is what my wife and I call an "easy meal" because this requires a small amount of ingredients and effort, perfect for any OT work days.

Ingredients:

  • 4 naan bread slices
  • 5 oz of hummus
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 avocado
  • 1 beefsteak tomato
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 1/2 stick salted butter
  • Salt, garlic, onion, pepper, and parsley

Steps:

  1. Heat pan to medium-high heat (5-7 on stove)
  2. While pan is heating up, mince garlic cloves and toss into bowl of softened butter with garlic and onion powder, pepper, and parsley.
  3. Spread mixture onto the 4 naan bread slices (only on 1 side of each).
  4. Put 1 tbsp of olive oil into pan, work it around the pan, then place naan bread onto pan with buttered side down. Leave for 3 min or adjust time based on preference.
  5. While the naan bread is cooking, slice tomato into 8 slices and avocado into 12.
  6. When the underside of the naan bread is done, transfer to plate and spread hummus on the cooked side, then place tomato and avocado slices on. Serve.
