Homemade Chicken Sandwich for 2 (Pan or air fried)

Description:

This chicken sandwich recipe is a staple in my home and never fails to satisfy our weekly Chick-Fil-A cravings (that somehow always happens every Sunday). Most of this recipe will be the same whether pan or air fried, but any differences will be specified in the "Steps" section.

Ingredients for chicken:

  • Chicken breast
  • 3 tablespoons of olive oil if air-fried or 4 tablespoons for pan-fried
  • Seasonings: salt, pepper, garlic, onion, paprika, cayenne, and parsley

For garlic butter:

  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 1/2 stick salted butter
  • Seasonings: garlic, onion, pepper, and parsley

For sandwich:

  • 1/4 of an onion
  • 1/2 of a beefsteak tomato
  • Handful of romaine lettuce
  • 8" of french bread or 2 pairs of brioche buns
  • Handful of shredded cheese or two slices of cheese

For sauce:

  • 2 parts mayo to 1 parts mayo, dijon mustard, hot sauce, worcestershire sauce, and ketchup
  • Salt, pepper, garlic, onion, and paprika

Instructions:

  1. Slice onions into strips and tomato into 1/4" slices.
  2. Start heating pan on medium heat (5-6 on stove).
  3. While pan is heating, mince garlic cloves and toss into softened (not melted) butter with some garlic powder, onion, pepper, and parsley. Adjust seasonings to taste.
  4. Cut off about 8" of french bread, cut longways to make 2 flat sides, then shortway again. If using brioche buns, grab 2 pairs.
  5. Spread garlic butter on flat sides of bread/bun until none of the white can be seen. Adding more garlic butter to bread/buns is acceptable.
  6. Cut chicken breast in half shortway and use 1 tbsp of oil to rub on both sides.
  7. Season both sides of cut chicken breasts with salt, pepper, garlic, onion, paprika, cayenne, and parsley.
  8. If air frying chicken, cook in air fryer at 400 degrees Fareinheit for 8-9 min, flipping halfway through. If pan frying, skip this step.
  9. Place a drop of oil in the hot pan, tilt around to cover surface, and place bread/bun on for 3 min. Adjust time based on preferences.
  10. While bread is cooking, mix mayo, dijon mustard, hot sauce, worcestershire sauce, and ketchup with salt, pepper, garlic, onion, and paprika. Adjust to taste.
  11. Set cooked bread/bun on plates and throw the sliced onion in the pan, stirring regularly. While onions are cooking, drizzle sauce on the bread/buns.
  12. Once onions are slightly translucent, add salt, pepper, garlic, onion, paprika, cayenne, and parsley.
  13. Keep stirring regularly for 2 min, then disperse onto bread/bottom buns, followed by lettuce and tomato slices.
  14. If air frying chicken, place cooked chicken on top of the veggies, followed by cheese, and serve. If pan frying, put 3 tbps of oil in pan and wait 1 min for oil to heat up (keep at medium heat).
  15. After 1 min has passed, place sliced chicken breast pieces in pan, cooking each side for 3 min before flipping. Keep flipping at 3 min time intervals until both sides are crispy and dark brown.
  16. Once chicken is done, place on bread/bottom buns
